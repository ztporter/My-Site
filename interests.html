If I could do anything I wanted, I would...

Have myself an adventure! Most of my favorite activities involve being outdoors and doing something active. There's just something about a cool breeze, a few trees, and being away from all the technology that crowds our lives that I find to be incredibly refreshing. Backpacking, camping, or hiking always give me a sense of freedom and peace that only comes from being out in the wild and far away from the hustle and bustle of the city. After taking a scuba diving class at UK I went on a trip with the folks at New Horizons dive shop and received my PADI Open Water certification, so now I'm a certified rec diver and plant to make full use of that. Breathing underwater and weightlessly floating through cool caves while fish are swimming past within arms reach was a pretty surreal experience for me, and I can't wait to go on another dive trip. I also enjoy almost any sport so long as it involves a healthy amount of running (I ran cross country and track in high school) and minimal contact (I'm not the largest fellow around). That being said, some of my favorite sports are definitely ultimate frisbee, soccer, basketball, volleyball, and baseball. I'm no pro at any of them but I enjoy playing in different rec leagues to get a little competition and have a good time with some friends. Not all adventures have to be outdoors though; I'm an avid reader of good books (mainly fantasy, theology, and mystery) and I thoroughly enjoy sitting in a cozy leather chair with a warm cup of tea and the occasional pipe, and letting my mind drift off to imagining strange new worlds and adventures. Overall, I'm convinced of the beauty of creation and the need for quiet prayer and honest reflection to help keep our lives in their proper perspective. Life is a great gift from a great God and I humbly try to never take mine for granted nor cease to enjoy it.
