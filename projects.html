Senior Design Project Team Rexacopter

I got to work along with my fellow teammates (Troy Fields, Mike Brancato, Michael Sandoval, and Mike Rudy) on designing a multi-rotor Unmanned Aerial Vehicle that was capable of aerially 3D mapping an archaeological dig site. The scope of our project was to design and build a functional system within two semesters (roughly 8 months). Our first phase of the project was focused on designing engineering requirements for the system such as the detail of the generated models, the duration for missions, the resolution of the imaging system, etc. After we had all of our requirements established we began researching smilar projects and the various subsystems that would make up the project including the imaging system, the UAV frame and mechanical parts, the autopilot software, and the ground station software. Once we had completed our research we finalized a parts list and purchased all necessary components and 3D printed some additional ones.
