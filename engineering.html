Electrical Engineering Qualifications

I have 2 years of professional experience in electrical and computer engineering, both as an electrical engineer and as a systems software developer. My strengths are:

  • Programming experience in C, C++, Python, Linux, Git, HTML, JavaScript.

  • Strong knowledge of embedded systems concepts (serial protocols, gpio port driving, etc.) and experience interfacing varius sensors and ICs with microcontrollers.

  • Familiarity with a variety of popular microcontrollers, including Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Intel Edison, ARM based microcontrollers, and others.

  • Experience using Altium to capture circuit schematics and design printed circuit boards.

  • Proficient at reading and understanding circuit schematics and data sheets for electrical components.

Some of the projects I've worked on are:

  • Designed and tested the electronics and software for the NanoRacks Feather Frame platform which flew a successful mission onboard the M6 launch of Blue Origin's New Shepard Vehicle. The platform receives flight data from the New Shepard vehicle, parses the data, and provides that reformatted data along with power to up to x12 2U sized experiments (roughly 10cm x 10cm x 20cm).

  • Prototyped and successfully tested on flight various iterations of an automated fluid mixing system based off of NanoRacks' MixStix experiments. Work involved digital circuit design, pcb layout and design, and embedded programming of the system.
