I'm currently an Electrical Engineer at NanoRacks LLC and a part time student in the MAPhil program at Houston Baptist University. I graduated, summa cum laude, from the University of Kentucky in 2015 with a BS in Electrical Engineering and a minor in mathematics.

My focus in electrical engineering is embedded systems, and I enjoy doing about anything related to electronics: digital circuit design, pcb design and layout, embedded programming, shell scripting in Linux, and higher level programming in C/C++, python, or whatever else I need to learn for a particular project.

I've also developed an increasing interest in philosophy through the courses I've taken at HBU. I initially enrolled in their MA in Apologetics program in 2015, but I found that I thoroughly enjoyed the philosophy courses I was taking and decided to switch tracks to Philosophy.
