Philosophy Education

I'm currently enrolled part-time in Houston Baptist University's MAPhil program, which I transferred into from their MA in Apologetics program. I completed two semesters of the MA in Apologetics before discovering that my real passion was philosophy, and I've been actively working towards my MAPhil the past two semesters and just recently got my major officially changed by the university.

Philosophy Courses Completed:

  • Problem of Evil - Course covered the history and development of the "problem of evil" along with various modern reformulations and responses to the problem.

  • Molinism - One week course on the Molinist account of divine providence.

  • Philosophy of Religion - Course focused on examining various theistic and atheistic arguments, the coherence of specific Christian doctrines, and the epistemology of religious belief.

  • Eschatology - Independent study with Dr. Jerry Walls focused on examining various accounts of Christian eschatology from a philosophical theology perspective.

  • Epistemology - Course focused primarily on discussing various accounts of knowledge along with examining other issues in modal epistemology, doxastic voluntarism, and virtue epistemology.
